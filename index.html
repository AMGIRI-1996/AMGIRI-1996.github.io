Skilled Engineer in various scientific programming tools and have a strong grounding in engineering, design and research. My interest is in exploring ways to improve the lives of people in developing countries with well-designed technological interventions.

I am working currently as Senior Software Engineer at Wadhwani Institute for Artificial Intelligence, an independent, non-profit research institute and global hub, developing AI solutions for social good. I have previously worked with startup focused on AR/VR camera applications and SDKs, drone data analysis and was also founding engineer at a startup in the home improvement space where I worked on building web based solutions that leveraged classical ML models.

Prior to that, I obtained my B.Tech at National Institute of Technology Warangal (NIT Warangal) where I founded an award (Design Challenge by Indian School of Business Hyderabad) winning startup which built a real time position reporting system for buses without using GPS.

I am also a robotics enthusiast and was a Fellow at Teach for India. Always an aspiring polymath!
